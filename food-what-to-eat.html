What to Eat in the United Kingdom

British foods are well-known for bad tastes, right? Well, the true answer is both. The United Kingdom is not well known for cooking things. However, the United Kingdom has wide varities of foods that are from other countries mixed. Fish and chips is the common typical British food to try. Is is comprised of fish fries and chips with the sauce. Bangers and Mash is the food comprised of sausages and mashed potatoes with the sauce. Bacon sandwich is made up of sandwich with the bacons inside of it.
