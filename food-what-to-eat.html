What to Eat in the United Kingdom

British food is well-known for its bad taste, right? Well, the true answers are yes and no. Why, the United Kingdom is known for having Gordon Ramsay? One of the greatest chefs in the world? Well, the food culture and industry were not developing well like countries like France and the United States. However, there are some great foods to eat while visiting the United Kingdom. Also, in the United Kingdom, you can experience a whole bunch of other foods from other countries around the world. In this page, great British foods are introduced. The first delicious British food you should try is ... Yeah, you probably already know it. It's Fish and Chips. Fish and Chips are widely well-known foods around the world. It's made up of very simple ingredients: potatoes and fish. Fish and Chips is fried fish with French Fries, with lime or lemon sauce blended. You eat Fish and Chips with ketchup. It's the food made for the workers of the factory during the Industrial Revolution. The second delicious food you should try is Bangers and Mash. Bangers and Mash are made of sausages and mashed potatoes with gravy. It's considered an extremely traditional meal. Since it has a long history. It's the simplest meal you can try to make at home. When you cook this meal, you shouldn't add the butter. You should add the butter after you finish cooking, since it makes a huge difference. The third delicious food you should try is English Breakfast. English Breakfast is similar to the American breakfast. Well, you know bacon, sausages, and scrambled eggs. However, this dish is added with tomatoes, mushrooms, and beans. It is for breakfast. Try making these foods after you visit the UK. Online, there are many guidelines you can follow to cook.
