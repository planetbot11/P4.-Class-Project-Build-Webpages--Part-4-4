Festivals in the United Kingdom

In the United Kingdom, there are a lot of festivals to enjoy when you vist the United Kingdom. These festivals will make you happy and provide you unforgettable memories with firends and family members. Now, let's explore! The first festival that I want to introduce to you is Jorvik Viking Festival. United Kingdom was once ruled by Vikings. The country that was ruled by Vikings was called Danelaw, and at that time, York was the capital city. In this festival, people celebrate the battle re-enactments by wearking historical costumes, such as big beard face wearing and hat with the horns. This festival happens on second week of February. The second festival that I want to introduce to you is Hogmanay. This is the celebration occurs at the last day of the year. This celebrates new year in Scotland. In this festival, there are many music concerts, games, and carnivals. During Hogmanay, people enter to other friends' houses to gift them cookies, shortbread, whiskey, and a cake. In Hogmanay, people wish others a good luck of next year. The third festival that I want to introduce to you is Notting Hill Carnival. This is a huge and glorious carnival that take place in the United Kingdom. This is an annual street carnival that takes on Notting Hill Street, London. This festival is celebrated on every August of the year. The fourth festival that I want to introduce to you is Wimbledon. Especially, if you like tennis, this festival is the best fir for you. This is the oldest tennis tournament in the world. In Wimbledon, the legendary tennis player Martina Navratilova won six consecutives single matches between 1982 and 1987.
